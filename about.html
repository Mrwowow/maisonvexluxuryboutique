ABOUT MAISON VEX

Luxury Fashion Since 2011

OUR STORY

Founded on September 18, 2011, Maison Vex Luxury Boutique has grown into one of Abuja's most prestigious destinations for international fashion. What began as a vision to bring world-class luxury to Nigeria has evolved into a fashion institution that defines elegance and sophistication.

Located at 42B Libreville Crescent, Wuse 2, Abuja, Maison Vex is more than just a boutique—it is a lifestyle hub where global luxury meets local elegance. Our carefully curated space offers an immersive shopping experience that celebrates both international haute couture and Africa's finest designers.

At its core, Maison Vex is dedicated to curating the world's most coveted fashion labels under one roof. Each piece is hand-selected to reflect the sophistication, craftsmanship, and exclusivity that define true luxury. From the runways of Paris and Milan to the creative studios of Lagos and Johannesburg, we bring together the best of global fashion.

THE VISIONARY

Aisha Bello Ibrahim, founder and creative director of Maison Vex, has built an empire of elegance with over a decade of experience in fashion retail and brand strategy. Her vision is clear: to create a space where African luxury consumers can discover, experience, and own international fashion without compromise.


"Fashion is not just about clothing; it's about confidence, expression, and celebrating one's unique style. At Maison Vex, we understand that luxury is personal, and we're committed to helping our clients find pieces that resonate with their individual aesthetic while maintaining the highest standards of quality and authenticity."

— Aisha Bello Ibrahim
